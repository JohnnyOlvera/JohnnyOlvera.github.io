Statement

I direct character-driven stories rooted in performance, tension, and emotional consequence. My work often explores moral pressure points—moments where people are forced to reveal who they are when the stakes are personal and irreversible.

Whether working in narrative features or select commercial projects, I approach each piece with the same discipline: grounded performances, clear visual intent, and a respect for the audience’s intelligence.
